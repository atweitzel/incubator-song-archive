INCUBATOR WIP Song Archive

This archive is a place for INCUBATOR artists to browse and submit demos, ideas, and unfinished songs to find collaborators. If you want to be involved, send your file(s) to wip@incubatorontheweb.com along with your name (or stage name, or band name), a brief description of the work, what you're looking for, and how to get in touch with you.

The project is entirely DIY from top to bottom. In support of the INCUBATOR philosophy, this project is intended to be open for contribution by the community of inspirational artists that make up the group. Anyone is welcome to make suggestions and contribute to this archive as it grows and changes.

Please submit files in .mp3, .wav, or .ogg format for best compatibility with web browsers.
